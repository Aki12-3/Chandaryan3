About CHANDRAYAAN-3
Congratulations🥳 CHANDRAYAAN 3 successfully softlanded on the lunar(Moon) surface.India is the first country to land on the south pole of the moon.Chandrayaan-3 is the third Indian lunar exploration mission under the Indian Space Research Organisation's (ISRO) Chandrayaan programme. It consists of a lander named Vikram and a rover named Pragyan, similar to those of the Chandrayaan-2 mission.
